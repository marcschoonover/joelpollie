Prometheus Confidential:

The Case of the Stolen Diamond

Reese Warren dives into a high-stakes investigation when a priceless diamond is stolen, uncovering layers of deception and betrayal.

Description

Reese Warren is no stranger to chaos—whether it’s the struggles of day-to-day life or the complications of an investigation that seems doomed from the start. When a dazzling, uncut diamond worth ten million dollars mysteriously disappears from the City Museum of Science, Reese is unexpectedly hired by Mary Campbell, the ambitious Head of Special Exhibits. Mary’s trust in Reese’s detective abilities is puzzling, but with the museum’s reputation hanging by a thread, there’s no time to question motives.
As the investigation unfolds, Reese uncovers a tangled web of suspects: an enigmatic former employee with a grudge, a team of guards guarding secrets of their own, and a volunteer staff whose motives remain unclear. Adding to the challenge are vivid, haunting dreams that might hold the key to cracking the case—if their meaning can be deciphered. With every lead uncovering more questions than answers, Reese must navigate reluctant witnesses, mounting suspicions, and a past filled with failure to unearth the truth.
Prometheus Confidential: The Case of the Stolen Diamond delivers twists, sharp humor, and a fresh take on the detective genre. Reese Warren may not be perfect, but in a mystery this convoluted, perfection is overrated.
